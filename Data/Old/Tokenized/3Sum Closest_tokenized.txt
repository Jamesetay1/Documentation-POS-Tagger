<p> Given an array <code> nums </code> of <em> n </em> integers and an integer <code> target </code> , find three integers in <code> nums </code> such that the sum is closest to <code> target </code> . 
Return the sum of the three integers . 
You may assume that each input would have exactly one solution . </p> 
<p> </p> 
<p> Example 1 : </p> 
<pre> Input : nums = [ - 1 , 2 , 1 , - 4 ] , target = 1 Output : 2 Explanation : The sum that is closest to the target is 2 . ( - 1 + 2 + 1 = 2 ) . </pre> 
<p> </p> 
<p> Constraints : </p> 
<code> 3 < = nums . length < = 10 ^ 3 </code> <code> - 10 ^ 3 < = nums [ i ] < = 10 ^ 3 </code> <code> - 10 ^ 4 < = target < = 10 ^ 4 </code>
