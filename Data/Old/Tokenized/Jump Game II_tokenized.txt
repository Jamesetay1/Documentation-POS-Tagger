<p> Given an array of non - negative integers <code> nums </code> , you are initially positioned at the first index of the array . </p> 
<p> Each element in the array represents your maximum jump length at that position . </p> 
<p> Your goal is to reach the last index in the minimum number of jumps . </p> 
<p> You can assume that you can always reach the last index . </p> 
<p> </p> 
<p> Example 1 : </p> 
<pre> Input : nums = [ 2 , 3 , 1 , 1 , 4 ] Output : 2 Explanation : The minimum number of jumps to reach the last index is 2 . 
Jump 1 step from index 0 to 1 , then 3 steps to the last index . </pre> 
<p> Example 2 : </p> 
<pre> Input : nums = [ 2 , 3 , 0 , 1 , 4 ] Output : 2 </pre> 
<p> </p> 
<p> Constraints : </p> 
<code> 1 < = nums . length < = 3 * 104 </code> <code> 0 < = nums [ i ] < = 105 </code>
